Allow background activity

Android may put this app to sleep to save battery. Disabling this ensures the bridge stays connected and delivers messages without delay.

⚠️ Without this, Android may delay message delivery by up to 15 minutes during Doze mode. The FCM fallback will still wake the app, but with higher latency.
